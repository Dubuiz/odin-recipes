Egusi-Soup

Ingredients

  • Ugu Leaves (African Spinach)
  • Pepper blended with crayfish
  • Ponmo (pork/beef-hide)
  • Egusi
  • Bonga Fish
  • Meat(Beef)
  • Palm oil
  • other spices

Recipe Instruction

  1. Season the meat with Maggi crayfish cubes and add little water for better absorbtion of the ingredients into the meat.
  2. Then added the Pomo with enough water to cook very well.
  3. Mash the egusi with some onions to form a dough.
  4. Add the ground yellow and red peppers with crayfish
  5. Then cut the dough and put inside the boiling stock, add palm oil and cover to cook
  6. When the soup was done, add the vegetable, stir for few seconds and bring it down.
