Agege-Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 Loaf of Agege bread
  • Pepper
  • 3 eggs
  • Milk
  • Onions

Recipe Instruction

  1. Dice the pepper and onions
  2. Break the eggs into a bowl
  3. Mix the pepper and onion into the egg, add salt or any desirable seasoning and beat
  4. fry the eggs
  5. Split the bread in two and soak into the milk
  6. Heat the bread in an oven for 5 mins, then add eggs in between the two halve
  7. Heat the sandwich in an oven and remove after 3 mins. Your sandwich is ready
