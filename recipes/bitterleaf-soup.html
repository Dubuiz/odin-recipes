Bitterleaf-Soup

Ingredients

  • Offor
  • Pepper blended with crayfish
  • Ponmo (pork/beef-hide)
  • Bitter-leaf
  • Ogiri
  • Meat(Beef)
  • Palm oil
  • other spices

Recipe Instruction

  1. Season the meat with Maggi crayfish cubes and add little water for better absorbtion of the ingredients into the meat.
  2. Cover and allow to simmer for some minutes. Add ponmo and more water to cook the meat soft.
  3. Add the ground yellow and red peppers with crayfish to the meat stock.
  4. Dissolve the ofor in palm oil with the ogiri, add to boiling meat stock and stir. Cover and allow to done.
  5. Add bitter leaf, let it cook for a few mins and finally add uziza leaves, stir and bring it down immediately.
